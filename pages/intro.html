Bike Share in San Francisco Bay Area


The data

This is a visualization application exploring a dataset regarding rental bike usage in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Throughout the Bay Area, there are bike stations containing a given number of docks and some bikes for usage.
Clients can use bikes and make trips from station to station.

There are two types of clients: customers and subscribers.


Visualizations

3 visualizations are available:

  • Geographic view: geographic data on a map of the Bay Area
  • City/Station details: data regarding a specific city or station
  • Global details: data regarding all cities and stations.

Filters are available in all visualizations, by hovering the mouse on the bottom of the page.
